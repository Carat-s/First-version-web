The Myers–Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is a personality framework that helps you understand your preferences in how you think, feel, and interact with the world. There are 16 types, each combining four dimensions: Extraversion–Introversion, Sensing–Intuition, Thinking–Feeling, and Judging–Perceiving.
